Calculators

Live updating GTO math — MDF, pot odds, optimal bluff frequency, and ICM equity.

MDF & Alpha

Defender frequency required to prevent a pure bluff from being profitable, and the bluff's required success rate.

Pot odds

Required equity to break even on a call.

Optimal bluff frequency

For a given bet size, how much of your betting range should be value vs bluff at equilibrium.

ICM Equity

Compute each player's $EV using the Malmuth-Harville model. Up to 9 players.

Tip: try 25, 25, 25, 25 with 100, 0, 0, 0 to see the winner-take-all distribution. The ICM tax on the chip leader is most visible when prizes are flat.
