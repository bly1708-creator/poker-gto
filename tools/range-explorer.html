How to read these ranges

The grid uses standard convention: rows and columns are A K Q J T 9 8 7 6 5 4 3 2.

  • Diagonal cells are pocket pairs (AA, KK, ..., 22).
  • Above the diagonal (top-right) are suited hands like AKs, AQs.
  • Below the diagonal (bottom-left) are offsuit hands like AKo, KQo.
  • Solid cells are pure actions; split cells are mixed strategies.

All ranges are simplified solver outputs at standard sizings (2.5× opens, 3-3.5× IP 3-bet, 4× OOP 3-bet, 100bb deep unless noted). For exact frequencies, run your own sims.
