What you'll actually learn

Read a range, not a hand

Stop thinking "what does he have" and start thinking "what class of hands does he have, and what is my EV against that distribution?"

Use frequencies, not absolutes

GTO answers come as mixed strategies. You'll learn which spots require 70/30 splits and why a pure strategy is almost always an exploit.

Know when to leave GTO

Equilibrium is the baseline. Once you understand it, every leak at your table becomes a dollar sign. We cover exploitative deviations too.
